Polaroid Cloud

Film to Phones

Any cloud, anytime.

With Polaroid Cloud, you can sync your photos to your favorite cloud service while still enjoying the special instant photo paper. Your image is uploaded to the web, and a QR code is generated and printed with it.

Easy, cheesy!

  • Connect your Polaroid to a WiFi network
  • Configure your favorite cloud service
  • Point, shoot, and print your QR-tagged photo
  • Share your physical and digital photos with friends and family
